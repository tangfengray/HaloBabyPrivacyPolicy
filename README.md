# Privacy Policy for Halo Baby - Sweet Baby Track
Last updated: February 2026
This privacy policy applies to the Halo Baby app (hereinafter referred to as "the App") developed by Xiaolei Wang (hereinafter referred to as "the Developer").

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The App only collects and stores user data locally on your device (including newborn feeding, sleep, diaper change records, etc.). **No user data is uploaded to the Developer's server or any third-party servers**, and no network connection is required for use.

## 2. Data Storage
All user data is stored locally on your mobile device. You can export the data at any time through the App's export function, and you have full control over all your data.

## 3. Data Sharing
The Developer will not share, sell, or transfer any of your local data to any third party.

## 4. Privacy Changes
The Developer may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated policy will be displayed in the App, and your continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
